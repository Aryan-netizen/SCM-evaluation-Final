Discover the Golden City of India

Jaisalmer, a land of desert, forts, and vibrant culture.

Top Attractions

  • Jaisalmer Fort: A UNESCO World Heritage site built with golden sandstone.
  • Patwon Ki Haveli: A cluster of five havelis showcasing beautiful architecture.
  • Gadisar Lake: A peaceful place with temples and boating opportunities.
  • Sam Sand Dunes: Popular for camel rides and desert safaris.

Top beautiful places

  • Bada Bagh: A serene garden complex with cenotaphs of Jaisalmer's rulers, offering stunning sunset views.
  • Kuldhara Village: A historic abandoned village with intriguing legends and a mysterious atmosphere.
  • Desert National Park: A vast expanse of sand dunes and wildlife, perfect for nature enthusiasts.
  • Khaba Fort: A ruined fort with panoramic views of the surrounding desert and a fascinating history.

Cultural Experiences

  • Rajasthani Folk Music and Dance: Enjoy mesmerizing performances of Kalbeliya and Ghoomar dances accompanied by traditional music.
  • Desert Camping: in luxurious tents under the starry desert sky.
  • Local Cuisine: Savor authentic Rajasthani dishes like Dal Baati Churma, Ker Sangri, and Gatte ki Sabzi.
  • Explore vibrant markets for exquisite textiles, jewelry, and leather goods crafted by local artisans.
